CCB Child Benefit (2025-2026)

  • Maximum Amount (per child):
  • Under 6: $7,997/year ($666.41/month)
  • 6-17 years: $6,748/year ($562.33/month)
  • Income ≤ $37,487: Full CCB, no reduction
  • Income $37,487 - $81,222 (Phase 1 Reduction):
  • 1 child: 7% | 2 children: 13.5%
  • 3 children: 19% | 4+ children: 23%
  • Income > $81,222 (Phase 2 Additional Reduction):
  • 1 child: $3,061 + 3.2%
  • 2 children: $5,904 + 5.7%
  • 3 children: $8,310 + 8%
  • 4+ children: $10,059 + 9.5%
